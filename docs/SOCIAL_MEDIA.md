# Flight Guardian - Social Media Launch Strategy

## Overview
This document outlines the social media strategy for the launch of Flight Guardian, focusing on engaging content that highlights our unique value proposition: "Precision Tracking, Real-time Intelligence."

## Platforms
- **Twitter/X:** Short, impactful updates, price drop alerts, tech insights, engagement with travel influencers.
- **LinkedIn:** Professional networking, technical deep-dives into Convex/Gemini integration, company updates.
- **Reddit (r/travel, r/flights, r/saas):** Community engagement, answering questions, sharing success stories (price saves), gathering feedback.

## Content Pillars
1. **Price Drop Alerts:** Real-time examples of flights where Flight Guardian identified significant savings.
2. **Tech Insights:** Behind-the-scenes on Convex, Gemini 3 Flash, and the "radar terminal" UI.
3. **Travel Tips:** General advice on optimal booking windows, flight hacking, and destination insights.
4. **User Testimonials:** Showcasing how Flight Guardian has saved users money and time.

## Hashtags
- #FlightGuardian #FlightTracker #CheapFlights #TravelDeals #PriceAlert #AIinTravel #Convex #Gemini3Flash

## Launch Timeline
- **Pre-Launch (1 month out):** Teaser content, waitlist promotion (using the new JoinWaitlistForm!), early access sign-ups.
- **Launch Day:** Official announcement across all platforms, press release, influencer collaborations.
- **Post-Launch (ongoing):** Regular content, community engagement, feature highlights, bug fixes (if any), continuous feedback loop.

## Key Message
"Never miss a deal again. Flight Guardian's AI-powered precision tracking ensures you always fly for less."
